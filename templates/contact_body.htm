Developer's notes

If you request to add a genome (or partial genome) of your interest, please include in your email the complete species name, the URL for accessing contiguous gene models and other genomes that you want to compare structure to. We will add the data once it is processed. We are also periodically checking the progress of major genome projects and will timely update the database (a rather complete list here).
